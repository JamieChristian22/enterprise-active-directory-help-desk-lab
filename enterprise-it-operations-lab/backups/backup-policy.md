# Backup Policy

| Tier | Systems | RPO | RTO | Retention |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Tier 1 | Identity, file, core application | 4 hours | 4 hours | 30 daily, 12 monthly |
| Tier 2 | Monitoring and internal services | 24 hours | 8 hours | 14 daily, 6 monthly |
| Tier 3 | Rebuildable lab systems | 24 hours | 24 hours | 7 daily |

Backups are encrypted, monitored, access-controlled, and tested. Critical backups include an isolated copy.
