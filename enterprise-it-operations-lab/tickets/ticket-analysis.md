# Resolved Ticket Analysis

This dataset contains **100 fully documented simulated operations tickets** covering identity, endpoint, Microsoft 365, networking, Windows/Linux infrastructure, AWS, and access management.

## Results
- SLA compliance: **95%**
- Mean resolution time: **245.8 minutes**
- Customer confirmation captured: **100%**
- Tickets with a root-cause statement: **100%**
- Highest recurring themes: identity, connectivity, endpoint compliance, service health, and storage capacity

## Operational interpretation
The queue demonstrates consistent triage, evidence-based troubleshooting, safe remediation, customer validation, and closure documentation. Recurring issues should feed knowledge management, monitoring improvements, and problem-management reviews.
