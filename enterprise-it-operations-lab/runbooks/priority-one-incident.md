# Runbook: Priority-One Incident

1. Declare the incident and record start time.
2. Assign incident commander, technical lead, communications lead, and scribe.
3. Create a dedicated incident channel and timeline.
4. Focus on service restoration; freeze unrelated changes.
5. Send updates every 30 minutes.
6. Validate recovery through monitoring and user testing.
7. Schedule RCA within two business days.
