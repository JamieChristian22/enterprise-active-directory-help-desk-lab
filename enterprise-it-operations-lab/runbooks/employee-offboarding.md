# Runbook: Employee Offboarding

1. Confirm approved termination time with HR and manager.
2. Disable sign-in in AD and Entra ID.
3. Revoke sessions, tokens, VPN, and MFA methods.
4. Remove privileged access and shared secrets.
5. Transfer mailbox, OneDrive, files, and application ownership.
6. Recover assets and update inventory.
7. Preserve data according to legal and retention requirements.
