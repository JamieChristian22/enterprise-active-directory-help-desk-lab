# SOP: Secure Server Build

## Objective
Provide a controlled, auditable process for secure server build across the simulated Northstar Services enterprise environment.

## Roles
- Requester: supplies business need and validation criteria
- Operator: performs documented work
- Approver: authorizes risk and production impact
- Reviewer: verifies evidence and closes control gaps

## Standard
1. Create a traceable request or scheduled work item.
2. Verify scope, owner, affected assets, business impact, risk, dependencies, and rollback.
3. Obtain required approval before production action.
4. Execute with named accounts and capture evidence.
5. Validate technical health and business outcome.
6. Update CMDB, documentation, ticket, and monitoring where applicable.
7. Record exceptions, residual risk, and follow-up owner/date.

## Controls
No shared administrator accounts; no undocumented production changes; no closure without evidence; separation of requester and approver for high-risk work.

## Metrics
Completion rate, SLA adherence, rework, failed-change rate, control exceptions, and aging of follow-up actions.
