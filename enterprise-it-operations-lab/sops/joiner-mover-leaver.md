# SOP: Joiner-Mover-Leaver

## Joiner
Create identity from approved HR record, assign role groups, issue licensed services, enroll device, enforce MFA, and obtain manager validation.

## Mover
Remove previous role access before adding new access. Preserve segregation of duties. Record manager and application-owner approvals.

## Leaver
Disable access at the approved time, revoke sessions, transfer data ownership, recover assets, and retain records.

## Evidence
Ticket, approvals, account timestamps, group changes, device assignment, and completion checklist.
