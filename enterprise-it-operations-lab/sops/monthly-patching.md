# SOP: Monthly Server Patching

1. Review vendor advisories and vulnerability priorities.
2. Test patches in the lab ring.
3. Approve change record with affected services and rollback plan.
4. Confirm backups and snapshots.
5. Patch development, then noncritical production, then critical production.
6. Reboot in approved windows.
7. Validate services, logs, monitoring, and business transactions.
8. Record compliance and exceptions.
