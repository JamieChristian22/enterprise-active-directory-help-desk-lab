# Business Scenario and Support Scope

## Organization

Northstar Professional Services employs 120 people. The organization operates Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time. Twenty-five employees work remotely at least three days per week.

## Supported services

The service desk supports Windows 11 Pro, Active Directory, Group Policy, DNS, DHCP, SMB file shares, network printers, VPN, Microsoft 365 authentication, BitLocker recovery coordination, browsers, Adobe Reader, and the Northstar CRM desktop client.

## Service desk model

- Tier 1 owns intake, identity verification, basic troubleshooting, documentation, and common fixes.
- Tier 2 owns advanced endpoint, Active Directory, Group Policy, file-permission, and application issues.
- Tier 3 or infrastructure engineering owns server outages, domain-controller health, firewall changes, backups, and widespread incidents.

## Priority definitions

| Priority | Definition | Initial response | Target resolution |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| P1 | Company-wide outage, security incident, or critical business service unavailable | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| P2 | Department-wide outage or executive unable to work | 30 minutes | 8 hours |
| P3 | Single user unable to perform a core task | 2 hours | 2 business days |
| P4 | Standard request, minor issue, or informational request | 4 hours | 5 business days |

## Identity verification policy

Before a password reset, MFA reset, BitLocker recovery disclosure, or access change, the technician must verify two approved factors: employee ID plus manager name, callback to a registered number, or an authenticated video call with badge display. Email alone is insufficient.
