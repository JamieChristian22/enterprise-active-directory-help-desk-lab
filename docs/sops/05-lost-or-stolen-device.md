# SOP: Lost or Stolen Device

1. Mark the ticket P1 when theft is suspected or sensitive data may be exposed.
2. Record asset tag, serial number, last known location, date, and circumstances.
3. Notify security and the user’s manager.
4. Disable or quarantine the computer object when directed.
5. Revoke VPN and cloud sessions.
6. Confirm BitLocker status and endpoint-management check-in.
7. Initiate remote lock or wipe through the authorized platform.
8. Preserve logs and do not erase evidence outside the approved process.
9. Arrange a replacement device after security approval.
