# SOP: Employee Offboarding

## Procedure
1. Confirm the termination ticket and effective time with HR.
2. Disable the AD account at the approved time.
3. Reset the password to a random unknown value.
4. Remove all active sessions and revoke cloud sign-in tokens.
5. Remove group memberships except the archival baseline.
6. Move the account to `Disabled Users`.
7. Transfer mailbox and OneDrive access only with written approval.
8. Remove VPN access and privileged roles.
9. Recover laptop, charger, badge, phone, and peripherals.
10. Archive the home folder according to retention policy.
11. Document every completed action and any unrecovered asset.

Never delete an account during initial offboarding.
