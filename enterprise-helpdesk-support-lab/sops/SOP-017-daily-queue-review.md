# SOP-017: Daily queue review

## Objective
Standardize daily queue review while protecting security, customer experience, and auditability.

## Procedure
1. Confirm request ownership, authorization, and business need.
2. Record required fields and supporting evidence.
3. Follow the approved workflow and least-privilege principle.
4. Validate the result with the requester or designated approver.
5. Document completion, exceptions, and follow-up tasks.

## Quality controls
No credentials are stored in tickets. Sensitive data is minimized. Administrative actions are traceable. Exceptions require escalation and approval.
