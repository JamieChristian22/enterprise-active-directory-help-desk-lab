# Service Desk Operating Model

## Priorities
- P1: widespread outage, security emergency, or critical business service unavailable
- P2: major degradation or multiple users blocked
- P3: single user blocked with workaround unavailable
- P4: minor issue, request, or informational task

## Targets
P1 response 10 minutes, restore 2 hours. P2 response 30 minutes, restore 4 hours. P3 response 4 business hours, resolve 2 business days. P4 response 1 business day, resolve 5 business days.

## Quality requirements
Every ticket must contain identity verification, impact, troubleshooting evidence, customer-facing updates, final resolution, root cause category, and prevention or follow-up when applicable.
