# RB-026: Certificate expiration response

## Trigger
Use this runbook when certificate expiration response is reported or detected.

## Actions
1. Open or update the incident and establish impact, urgency, and affected scope.
2. Verify identity and authorization before any account or device action.
3. Check service health, monitoring, recent changes, and known errors.
4. Capture timestamps, logs, screenshots, device IDs, and network details.
5. Apply approved containment or restoration steps.
6. Escalate with evidence when the documented threshold is met.
7. Validate service restoration with a technical test and user confirmation.
8. Record timeline, root-cause category, communication, and follow-up.

## Rollback
Reverse only the change introduced by the response, restore the last known-good configuration, and notify the incident lead.

## Completion criteria
Impact is removed, monitoring is stable, the requester or incident lead confirms recovery, and documentation is complete.
