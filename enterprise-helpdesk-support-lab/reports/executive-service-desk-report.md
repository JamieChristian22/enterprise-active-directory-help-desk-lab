# Executive Service Desk Report

The simulated service desk demonstrates strong queue governance, repeatable troubleshooting, and customer-focused communication. The included datasets support reporting for SLA attainment, first-contact resolution, customer satisfaction, backlog, ticket volume, category trends, endpoint compliance, asset status, access-request turnaround, and phishing response.

Recommended management actions: automate repetitive identity and endpoint checks, review top recurring ticket categories monthly, maintain a published knowledge base, audit ticket quality, and convert recurring incidents into problem records.
