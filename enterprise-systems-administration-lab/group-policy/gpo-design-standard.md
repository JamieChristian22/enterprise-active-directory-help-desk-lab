# Group Policy Design Standard
- Separate baseline, role, application, and exception policies.
- Use descriptive naming and documented ownership.
- Link at the narrowest appropriate OU.
- Avoid broad Enforced and Block Inheritance usage.
- Test in a pilot OU before production rollout.
- Back up every GPO before modification.
- Record WMI filters, security filtering, loopback mode, dependencies, rollback, and validation commands.
