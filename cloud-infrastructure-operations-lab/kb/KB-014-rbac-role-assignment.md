# KB-014: RBAC role assignment

## Symptoms
An operator or user reports an issue related to rbac role assignment.

## Diagnostic Steps
1. Verify identity, resource, region, and timestamp.
2. Reproduce safely or inspect monitoring evidence.
3. Review recent changes, logs, permissions, networking, and quotas.

## Resolution
Correct the identified configuration or service issue, then validate with an independent health check.

## Prevention
Use monitoring, policy controls, automation, and peer-reviewed change management to reduce recurrence.
